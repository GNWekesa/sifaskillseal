Our Founding Story

SifaSkills (EA) Ltd (SEAL) was born from a shared vision and a deep understanding of the challenges facing youth and businesses across East Africa...

  • Students graduating with academic qualifications but lacking practical, employable skills
  • Startups and small businesses struggling to grow due to limited access to expert guidance

To break this cycle, we established SEAL with a clear mission: to equip students and recent graduates with job-ready skills...

Why SEAL?

The name SEAL represents our core purpose:

  • Strengthening Communities
  • Empowering Entrepreneurs
  • Advancing Education & Skills
  • Leading Innovation
