About SifaSkills (EA) Ltd

SifaSkills (EA) Ltd (SEAL) is a forward-thinking company committed to unlocking human potential and driving sustainable development through skills enhancement, innovative solutions, and impactful partnerships.

Our Vision

To build a future-ready East Africa where youth are empowered with practical, employable skills and businesses thrive through expert guidance, innovation, and sustainable growth.

To be East Africa's leading catalyst for personal and enterprise transformation — connecting talent with opportunity and enabling communities, institutions, and businesses to achieve their fullest potential.

Our Mission

  • •Equip individuals with job-ready skills and support SMEs in achieving sustainable growth
  • •Bridge the gap between education and employability
  • •Design impactful programs that foster entrepreneurship
  • •Collaborate with partners to build a skilled workforce
